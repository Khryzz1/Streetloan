About Street Loan

Empowering Ghanaians with fair, transparent collateral lending since 2018.

Our Story

Street Loan was founded in Accra with a simple belief: hardworking Ghanaians should be able to access credit using assets they already own — especially their vehicles — without unfair terms or confusing paperwork.

Today we serve customers from Osu to Kejetia, with a focus on vehicle-backed loans while also supporting property and valuable-asset collateral. We speak your language, understand local markets, and disburse in Ghana Cedis.

Mission

To provide accessible collateral loans that help Ghanaians grow businesses, handle emergencies, and build financial stability.

Integrity

Vehicle First

Cars are our specialty. We employ certified valuers who understand the Ghanaian auto market.
