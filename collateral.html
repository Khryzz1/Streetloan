What Can You Use as Collateral?

Cars are our specialty — but we also accept houses, land, and other high-value assets across Ghana.

Most Popular

Vehicle Collateral

Use your saloon, SUV, pickup, minibus, or commercial vehicle to secure a loan. We accept locally registered vehicles with valid roadworthy certificates.

  • Borrow up to 70% of vehicle value
  • Keep driving your car during repayment*
  • Toyota, Honda, Mercedes, Kia, and more
Apply with Your Car

Private Cars

Personal vehicles from 2010 onwards. Minimum loan ₵5,000.

Taxi & Commercial

Trotro, taxi, and delivery fleet vehicles. Weekly repayment available.

SUVs & Pickups

Higher loan amounts for Land Cruiser, Hilux, and similar models.

Property Collateral

Residential homes, apartments, commercial buildings, and titled land in Ghana can secure larger loan amounts with lower interest rates.

  • Up to 60% of property valuation
  • Title deed or lease documentation required
  • Longer repayment terms (up to 60 months)
Apply with Property

Other Valuables

Gold jewellery, electronics, industrial equipment, and inventory for traders. Ideal for short-term business needs.

  • Certified gold valuation partners
  • Secure storage during loan period
  • Flexible 3–24 month terms
Apply with Valuables
