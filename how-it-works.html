How Street Loan Works

From online application to cash in your account — typically within 24–48 hours in Ghana.

  1. Apply Online or Visit a Branch

    Fill out our application form with your details and collateral information. Choose vehicle, property, or valuables. No upfront payment required.

  2. Collateral Valuation

    Our certified valuers inspect your car, property, or items. For vehicles, we check condition, mileage, and market value in the Ghanaian auto market.

  3. Receive Your Offer

    We present a clear loan offer in GHS — amount, interest rate, repayment schedule, and all fees. You decide whether to accept. No pressure.

  4. Sign Agreement & Disbursement

    Sign your contract at a branch or via our digital process. Funds are sent to your bank account or mobile money (MTN, Telecel, AirtelTigo).

  5. Repay on Your Schedule

    Make monthly or weekly payments via mobile money, bank transfer, or at any Street Loan branch. Early repayment discounts available.

What You Need to Apply

  • Valid Ghana Card (national ID)
  • Proof of address (utility bill or tenancy)
  • Collateral documents (vehicle registration, land title, etc.)
  • Recent passport photograph
  • Bank account or mobile money number for disbursement

For vehicle loans, bring your car for inspection at our valuation centre or request a mobile valuer (selected areas).
